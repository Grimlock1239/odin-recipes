Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious Melt in Your Mouth Cookies

Whether your having company over or you just want a snack these cookies are going to wow anyone who wants to try them.

Simple and easy to make. Takes 30 minutes to whip these puppies up and only 15 minutes of bake time. You will be having a wonderful ride through the delicious chocolate wonderland!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Salted Butter
  • 1 Cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (14 oz)
