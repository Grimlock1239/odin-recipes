The Greatest Lemon Tea Ever

Its a cool fall evening and your on the porch. Your wife has just walked out from inside the kitchen, the sunset just resting on her face. As her face glows in the sunlight you notice a glisten in your eyes coming from her hand. Low and behold its her world famous Lemon Tea.

Does this story sound dreamy? Well get ready to make it a reality with this awesome reicpe!
